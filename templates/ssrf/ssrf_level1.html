Challenge Description

Welcome to WebFetch Pro, a fictional URL fetching service! This challenge simulates a real-world scenario where a URL fetcher is vulnerable to Server-Side Request Forgery (SSRF).

The developers of WebFetch Pro have created a simple service that allows users to fetch content from any URL. However, they've made a critical mistake in how they handle user input, allowing attackers to make requests to internal services.

Your Task: Exploit the SSRF vulnerability to access internal services and retrieve the flag.

Hint: Think about what happens when you try to fetch content from internal addresses like localhost or private IP ranges.

Tools: You can use Burp Suite to intercept and modify requests, or simply test different URLs in the form below.
