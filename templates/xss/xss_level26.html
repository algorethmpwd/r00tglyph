Welcome to Multi-Context Validator.

Objective

Create a payload that executes in multiple contexts (HTML, JS, URL) simultaneously.

A polyglot XSS payload is a string of characters that breaks out of multiple different contexts (HTML attribute, script string, etc.) and executes valid JavaScript.

Use combinations like javascript://%250Aalert(1)//" to satisfy URL schemes, comment out trailing code, and break out of strings.

Example: javascript://%250Aalert(1)//"></script>
